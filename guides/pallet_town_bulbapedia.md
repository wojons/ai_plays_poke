# Pokémon Red & Blue Walkthrough — Part 1 (Bulbapedia)

Source: https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Walkthrough:Pokémon_Red_and_Blue/Part_1

## Controls
- **D-pad**: Move character, move cursor in menus
- **A**: Interact with people/environment, select menu options
- **B**: Cancel selection, exit menu, DELETE previous letter in name entry
- **START**: Open main menu, confirm name (select "End")

## Name Entry
- On name keyboard: A selects a letter, B deletes previous letter
- Navigate keyboard grid with d-pad, press A to pick each letter
- Press START or select "End" in bottom-right when done
- Max 7 characters

## Pallet Town — Step by Step

### 1. Bedroom (2F)
- Start facing the SNES/TV
- Walk to PC in corner → Withdraw Item → take **Potion** (adds to Bag)
- Press B to close menus
- Stairs in **northeast corner** → go downstairs

### 2. Downstairs (1F)
- Mom at table → tells you Oak is looking for you
- Floor mat along south wall → press DOWN to leave house

### 3. Pallet Town
- 3 buildings: your house (west), rival's house (east), Oak's lab (south)
- Head **north** toward tall grass (Route 1)

### 4. Oak Appears
- Oak stops you: "It's dangerous without a Pokémon!"
- Takes you to his lab

### 5. Oak's Lab — Choose Starter
- Rival (Blue) is already waiting
- Three Poké Balls on table:
  - **Bulbasaur** (Grass/Poison) — Good vs Water/Rock/Ground, weak to Fire/Flying/Psychic/Ice. Best for beginners.
  - **Charmander** (Fire) — Good vs Grass/Bug/Ice, weak to Water/Rock/Ground. Hard mode early.
  - **Squirtle** (Water) — Good vs Fire/Rock/Ground, weak to Grass/Electric. Most well-rounded.
- Rival picks the one with type advantage over yours

### 6. Rival Battle — Level 5
| Your pick | Rival gets | Rival's moves |
|-----------|-----------|---------------|
| Bulbasaur | Charmander (Fire, L5) | Scratch (Normal), Growl (Normal) |
| Charmander | Squirtle (Water, L5) | Tackle (Normal), Tail Whip (Normal) |
| Squirtle | Bulbasaur (Grass/Poison, L5) | Tackle (Normal), Growl (Normal) |

- Use Potion if HP runs low (Bag → Item → Potion)
- Win → earn 175 PokéDollars, Pokémon grows to Level 6

### 7. Leave for Route 1
- After battle, rival leaves
- Exit lab, head north to Route 1

## Items Available
- **Potion**: PC in bedroom (start)
- **Pokédex**: Oak's Lab (later, after parcel delivery)
- **Town Map**: Rival's house from Daisy (after Pokédex)
