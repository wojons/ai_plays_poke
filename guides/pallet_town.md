# Pokémon Blue Walkthrough — Pallet Town

Source: StrategyWiki (https://strategywiki.org/wiki/Pokémon_Red_and_Blue/Pallet_Town)

## Sequence

1. **Bedroom** — Start in your room. Grab the Potion from your PC (press A on the computer). Mom heals your Pokémon for free anytime.
2. **Downstairs** — Walk down the stairs, talk to Mom (she tells you Oak is looking for you).
3. **Outside** — Exit the house. Pallet Town is small: your house, rival's house, Oak's lab.
4. **North toward Route 1** — Walk north into the tall grass. Oak stops you — "It's dangerous without a Pokémon!"
5. **Oak's Lab** — Oak takes you back. Your rival is already there. Oak offers you a starter:
   - **Bulbasaur** (Grass/Poison) — Good vs first 3 gyms, beginner-friendly
   - **Charmander** (Fire) — Hard mode early, learns Flamethrower at lv46
   - **Squirtle** (Water) — Balanced, learns Ice moves via TM
6. **Rival Battle** — Your rival picks the starter with type advantage over yours. He pays 175 if you win.
7. **Leave for Route 1** — After the battle, head north to Route 1 to begin your journey.

## Key Screens

- **Bedroom**: Checkered floor, PC on north wall, plant to right, stairs to left
- **Oak's Lab**: Diagonal-stripe floor, PC terminal, plant, cabinet, Oak + rival NPCs
- **Starter selection**: Three Poké Balls on a table, text prompts for choice
- **Rival battle**: Battle screen with starter Pokémon, HP bars
