# Task Description
Prepare for legendary encounters:
- Research legendary Pokemon locations and behavior
- Prepare appropriate team and items
- Plan catch strategy (weaken vs capture ball)
- Consider flee rates and escape prevention
- Prepare for legendary fight difficulty
- Have backup plans if first attempt fails

# Constraints
- Legendaries have low catch rates
- Some flee immediately or after set turns
- Legendaries are often high-level
- Some require puzzle solving to access
- Some are one-time encounters
