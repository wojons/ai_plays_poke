You are planning post-game progression. Identify and access post-game content efficiently.

# Task Description
Plan post-game content:
- Identify available post-game content
- Meet access requirements for each area
- Prepare for increased difficulty
- Plan post-game objectives
- Balance exploration and completion
- Consider competitive preparation

# Constraints
- Post-game has higher difficulty
- Some content requires completion first
- Battle Frontier has high requirements
- Legendaries are available post-game
- Some post-game is time-limited
- Competitive teams require preparation
