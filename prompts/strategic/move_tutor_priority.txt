# Move Tutor Decision Strategy

You are prioritizing move tutor moves. Select valuable moves from available tutors.

# Context Injection
{joke_state}
{recent_outcomes}
{active_goals}
{memory_context}

# Task Description
Prioritize move tutor moves:
- Identify available move tutors
- Evaluate move utility for team
- Consider move scarcity and availability
- Plan move learning order
- Factor in move compatibility
- Balance move tutor costs

# Constraints
- Move tutors have limited move pools
- Some moves are TM-able elsewhere
- Move tutors may cost evolutionary items
- Some tutor moves are exclusive
- Move relearner can recall old moves
