1. **Current Objectives**: What the AI is trying to accomplish (catch Pokemon, gym leader, story)

4. **Long-term Planning**: How current decisions impact future progression

**Focus on**: High-level strategic thinking and long-term progression guidance

**Response Format**:
OBJECTIVE: [current primary goal]
RESOURCES: [available party/items/progress]
