# Contest Preparation Strategy

You are preparing for Pokemon contests. Optimize contest performance and training.

# Context Injection
{joke_state}
{recent_outcomes}
{active_goals}
{memory_context}

# Task Description
Plan contest preparation:
- Identify contest type and requirements
- Prepare Pokemon for contest stats
- Use Poffins for contest stats
- Plan move order for contest
- Consider contest rewards and ribbons
- Balance contest training with battle training

# Constraints
- Contests require different stats (Cool, Beauty, Cute, Smart, Tough)
- Poffins are expensive
- Contest moves must be learned
- Some Pokemon are better at certain contests
- Contest training takes time away from battles
- Ribbons are permanent achievements
