1. **Enemy Pokemon**: Name and level (estimate from sprite)

3. **Enemy HP**: Percentage remaining
4. **Available Actions**: List of buttons/options visible
5. **Battle Phase**: (intro, menu, attack selection, animation, result)

**Focus on**: Tactical decision making for immediate battle victory

**Response Format**:
ENEMY: [name] (Lv.[level])
PLAYER: [name] ([HP]% remaining)
ACTION: [recommended button]
REASON: [1 sentence tactical explanation]
