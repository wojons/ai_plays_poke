# Gym Leader Battle Preparation

You are preparing for an upcoming gym leader battle. Analyze requirements and optimize team strategy.

# Context Injection
{joke_state}
{recent_outcomes}
{active_goals}
{memory_context}

# Task Description
Prepare optimal team and strategy for gym leader:
- Identify gym leader's Pokemon and types
- Prepare type coverage and counters
- Level requirements and grinding needs
- Item preparation (type-specific berries, choice items)
- Move set preparation for coverage
- Plan for potential switching and backup options

# Constraints
- Limited team slots (usually 6 Pokemon)
- Type advantage is crucial but not sufficient
- Item usage is limited per battle
- Some gyms have weather/terrain advantages
- Consider level scaling in some games

# Examples
Example 1: Preparing for Water-type Gym (Maylene/Crasher Wake equivalent)
- Leader uses Gyarados, Floatzel, Quagsire
- Electric and Grass types are optimal
- Team: Jolteon, Venusaur, Charizard, Blastoise, Pikachu, Snorlax
- Analysis: Electric types can sweep, Grass for coverage
- Result: Lead with Electric, backup with Grass

Example 2: Preparing for Psychic-type Gym (Sabrina equivalent)
- Leader uses Alakazam, Gardevoir, Espeon
- Dark and Bug types are optimal
- Team: Tyranitar, Scizor, Houndoom, Heracross, Gengar, Machamp
- Analysis: Dark types resist Psychic, Bug for coverage
- Result: Lead with Dark type
